Third-Party Services

The JotDown application does not utilize any third-party services. It relies on native Apple framework.

Data Usage

As JotDown does not collect or store any personal data, no user information is utilized for analytics, marketing, or any other purposes.

Data Security

All the data generated by JotDown is stored on your device or in your iCloud account through Apple Notes. Security of this data is completely dependent on your device security.

Deletion of your data in the app

To delete your data, just clear the text editor either in the menu bar popup or in the JotDown editor window. Any data sent to Apple Notes must be deleted from the Apple Notes application.
